About

MinTube Android App

Floating Player

Play YouTube in a floating player that can be minimized and moved around the screen.

Easy Play

Easily play a single YouTube video or an entire YouTube playlist just by click.

Share to app

Share YouTube videos or playlists from youtube app to MinTube to play in background while doing other tasks.

Handle Links

YouTube video and playlist links are also handled by the app. You can open a YouTube link in MinTube to work parallelly.

Features

MinTube is packed with features like

User Settings

User can contorl most of the aspects of the app.

Video Quality

Video Playback Quality can be controlled.

Video/Playlist Repeat

A single video or playlist can be set on repeat.

View screen Size

View window size can be changed.

Search

Search and watch your favorite videos.

User Login

Login to YouTube and access your data.

Notification

Control Video playback via notification.
